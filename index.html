Things to do in Bergen

  • Fløyen: Amazing view – take the Fløibanen or hike up
  • Ulriken: Highest mountain in Bergen – take the cable car or hike the trail
  • Fish Market: Local food and seafood – best during the day
  • KODE Museums: Art and culture in the city center
  • Bryggen: UNESCO heritage site, historic wooden buildings and charming alleys
  • Restaurants: Try Lysverket, Colonialen, or Bare Vestland
  • Cafés: Kaffemisjonen, Blom, or Det Lille Kaffekompaniet

Check-out

  • Start the dishwasher if it’s at least half full
  • Turn off lights and air conditioning
  • Double-check you’ve packed everything (closets, bathroom, fridge)
  • Lock the door and return the key to the key box
